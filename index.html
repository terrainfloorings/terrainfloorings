Terrain® Floorings is a global manufacturer, supplier and installer of made-to-order rubber flooring solutions.

Established in 1994, Terrain® Floorings is an ISO 9001 certified manufacturer and installer of resilient rubber flooring solutions that deliver well-being and performance to our users. All Terrain® products are made to international specifications in our state-of-the-art facility in the UAE using raw materials from reputed European suppliers. Our commitment to quality and proven track record has made us the partner of choice for architects, engineers, interior designers and flooring system providers across the GCC (United Arab Emirates, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Bahrain, Kuwait and Oman) and beyond.

Types of Floorings

  • TERRAIN® | ECO

    Made with high quality recycled rubber granules & raw materials from reputed European manufacturers, Terrain® Eco products are comfortable, durable and provide excellent impact absorption.

  • TERRAIN® | VULCAN

    Made with superior quality NR-SBR (Natural and Styrene Butadiene Rubber), Terrain® Vulcan products are resistant to abrasion while giving users great comfort and protection.

  • TERRAC™

    The premium solution to the problem of noise transmission in multi-storied buildings. Terrac recycled rubber underlay provides optimal sound reduction while remaining impervious to the elements.

  • SEAMLESS FLOORING

    New generation flooring without ridges or joints, our Seamless systems are installed on-site and provide a great mix of durability and aesthetics.

  • OTHER FLOORING

    Looking for resilient flooring solutions that don’t fall into any of our other product categories? Find them here.

FAQs

Rubber flooring can be built of synthetic or natural materials, including recycled tyres. Because of its elasticity and bounce, it falls under the category of resilient flooring.

Our regular dimensions for the Eco tiles are 50x50 or 100x100. And the thickness starts from 15mm to 70mm. However, since we are manufacturers of these products, any customization can be possible.

We usually maintain stock for the regular items. However, on average we should take 7-10 days to produce 100 sqm of tiles. (For a specific enquiry, feel free to get in touch with us for the accurate lead time. It could be ready in stock or can take within 2-3 days to deliver.)

Eco and Vulcan flooring: 1-2 days
Seamless flooring: 3-5 days
Rubber rolls: 2-4 days

Color fading is a normal process due to exposure to UV radiation. However, for outdoor applications, we use a special material to limit the yellowing effect on the flooring and increase the longevity of the product.

An initial smell in the rubber products is normal. However, with enough ventilation post installation, the smell should go away. If it doesn’t, feel free to get back to us. We will work out a solution for you.

We offer a warranty of 2 years for all Terrain manufactured products.

We offer a variety of test reports for our products. For more information, please visit the Downloads section of our website.

We specialize in in various rubber tiles, rubber mats & technical rubber products, developed to the exact standards of international specifications.
